## üö® Enforcement & Violations

### How We Enforce This Policy

**Our approach:**

1. **Education First** - We prefer to inform rather than litigate
2. **Cease & Desist** - Formal request to stop infringing use
3. **UDRP/Legal Action** - Last resort for serious violations

**Timeline:**

1. **Discovery** - We notice potential violation
2. **Investigation** - Verify facts
3. **Contact** - Reach out to discuss (7 days to respond)
4. **Resolution** - Agree on corrective action
5. **Escalation** - Legal action if no cooperation

**We're reasonable.** Most cases resolve through friendly discussion.

### What Happens If You Violate

**Minor Violations:**

- Polite email requesting correction
- Transition period to comply (typically 30 days)
- No penalties if corrected promptly

**Example:**

> "Hi! We noticed you're using 'anchorpipe' in your domain name. Could we discuss alternative branding? We're happy to work with you on a solution."

**Moderate Violations:**

- Formal cease & desist letter
- Request to cease use within 14 days
- May request compensation for damages (if applicable)

**Severe Violations:**

- UDRP complaint (for domain disputes)
- Legal action (trademark infringement)
- Injunction to stop use
- Damages and legal fees

**What's "severe"?**

- Intentional confusion with official project
- Commercial exploitation causing harm
- Refusal to cooperate after contact
- Bad faith domain squatting

### Reporting Violations

**See potential trademark violation?**

**Report to:** governance@anchorpipe.dev

**Include:**

- URL or location of violation
- Description of infringing use
- Evidence (screenshots, links)
- Why you believe it's a violation

**We review all reports** but may not act on every one (discretion applied).

### Good Faith Safe Harbor

**If you:**

- ‚úÖ Act in good faith
- ‚úÖ Correct use promptly when notified
- ‚úÖ Didn't intentionally violate

**We won't:**

- ‚ùå Seek damages
- ‚ùå Pursue legal action
- ‚ùå Publicly shame you

**Mistakes happen.** We care more about correction than punishment.
