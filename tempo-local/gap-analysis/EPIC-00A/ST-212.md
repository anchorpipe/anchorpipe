# Gap Analysis: ST-212 - Implement Compliance Documentation

   - ✅ Security measures and sub-processors
   - ✅ Data subject rights and breach notification
   - ✅ Public-facing page: `apps/web/src/app/legal/dpa/page.tsx`

3. **Data Retention Policy**
   - ✅ Comprehensive Retention Policy: `docs/reference/compliance/retention-policy.md`
   - ✅ Retention periods by data category:

     - Audit and Security Data

   - ✅ Deletion procedures
   - ✅ Configuration options

4. **Public-Facing Documentation Pages**
   - ✅ Privacy Policy page: `apps/web/src/app/privacy/page.tsx`

   - ✅ Links to all compliance documents
   - ✅ Overview of compliance framework

6. **DSR Workflow Documentation**
   - ✅ Data Subject Request (DSR) workflow documented in Privacy Policy
   - ✅ GDPR Article 15-22 compliance
   - ✅ Request procedures and response times
   - ✅ Contact information for DSR requests

7. **Review Cadence and Ownership**
   - ✅ Review cadence defined (annual or as needed)
   - ✅ Ownership assigned (Legal/Compliance team)
   - ✅ Update procedures documented
